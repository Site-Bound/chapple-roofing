A Subcontractor You Can Trust to Deliver

Why Choose Us

What Commercial Clients Get From Us

We've worked with enough commercial projects to understand exactly what matters on site — and to structure our service accordingly.

  • Reliable lead times — we commit to manufacture and delivery schedules that work within your programme, and we communicate proactively if anything changes.
  • Full compliance documentation — every installation comes with test certificates, a BS 6180 compliance statement, and O&M manual content ready to hand over.
  • Dedicated project management — a single point of contact from quotation to handover. No chasing different departments for updates.
  • CAD and shop drawing service — we work from your architectural drawings and produce fabrication drawings for sign-off before manufacture begins.
  • National UK coverage — our installation teams operate across England, Wales, and Scotland. No project is too geographically remote or too large in scope.
01

Programme-Aware Scheduling

We understand that glass installation is a finishing trade. We work with your site manager to schedule around preceding works — so we're never the reason your handover date slips.

02

Specification Compliance

All installations are delivered to BS 6180 and UK Building Regulations Part K. We'll review your project specification prior to quoting and flag any items that require design input.

03

Design Coordination

We're experienced at working within design-and-build and traditional procurement routes. We can produce shop drawings independently or collaborate with your project architect.

04

Clean Site Practice

Our installation teams work to high standards of site discipline — CSCS-carded operatives, tool-box talks, risk assessments, and method statements provided as standard on all commercial contracts.
