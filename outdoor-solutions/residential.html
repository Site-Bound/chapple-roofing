Why Glass

A Home Improvement That Pays For Itself

Adding a glass balustrade to your home is one of the most impactful improvements you can make. Unlike timber or metal alternatives, glass does something entirely unique — it opens a space up. Light flows through, views remain unobstructed, and the result feels more spacious, more modern, and considerably more valuable.

Whether you're finishing a new-build, renovating an existing property, or extending into the garden, we'll help you find the right glass balustrade solution for your space — and fit it to a standard you'll be proud of for decades.

Applications

Where We Install

Garden & Decking

Replace timber or wrought iron railings with a frameless glass system and the transformation is immediate. Light floods the space, your garden view is fully restored, and the boundary between inside and outside almost disappears. All metalwork is marine-grade stainless steel — built for the UK climate.

Staircases

A glass staircase balustrade replaces the traditional spindle-and-newel arrangement with clean panels of toughened glass. The staircase becomes a feature in its own right — light, open, and architecturally striking. We work with straight, turned, and curved staircases across all house types.

Juliet Balconies

If your bedroom or living area has a door or window at first floor height, a Juliet balcony opens the space to the outside without the cost or planning requirements of a full projecting balcony. Our frameless Juliet systems typically install in a single day and immediately transform how your room feels.
