Our Story

About Bespoke Blinds & Shutters

We're a family-run business based right here in Formby, and we're passionate about one thing: transforming homes with beautifully crafted, made-to-measure window treatments. From our first consultation to the moment the last bracket is in place, we put your home and your comfort at the centre of everything we do.

As a local Merseyside business, we understand the homes, the styles, and the people in our community. We don't do call centres or cookie-cutter solutions — just honest advice, quality products, and workmanship you can trust, from people who genuinely care.

Whether you're looking for the timeless elegance of plantation shutters, the clean practicality of roller blinds, or the soft warmth of Roman blinds, we'll guide you through every step — from choosing the right fabric to professional fitting at a time that suits your schedule.
