Service Agreement

June 2026  ·  Credvanta Recovery Group Limited

1. Parties

This Agreement is entered into between Credvanta Recovery Group Limited (company number 17251971), with its registered office at 5 Canon Court, Institute Street, Bolton, BL1 1PZ, England, and operating from Suite 2/3, 48 West George Street, Glasgow, G2 1BP (“Credvanta”) and the Client instructing Credvanta to provide debt recovery services.

2. Purpose of Agreement

This Agreement sets out the basis on which Credvanta will provide commercial debt recovery services to the Client. It is to be read alongside Credvanta’s Terms & Conditions, which form part of this Agreement.

3. Services

Credvanta agrees to provide the Client with commercial debt recovery services as set out in its Terms & Conditions, including pre-legal collections, Letters Before Action, negotiation, field visits (where instructed), trace & locate, and referral to legal representatives.

Credvanta will use reasonable endeavours to recover the Debt(s) submitted by the Client but makes no guarantee as to the outcome of any recovery action.

4. Commercial Debts Only

This Agreement applies exclusively to commercial business-to-business (B2B) debts. The Client confirms that all debts submitted under this Agreement are commercial debts owed by businesses and not by individual consumers.

Credvanta does not carry out regulated consumer credit activity and is not authorised or regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA).

5. Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information in respect of each Debt;
  • Notify Credvanta promptly of any direct payment received from a Debtor or any dispute raised;
  • Comply with applicable data protection law in sharing Debtor information with Credvanta;
  • Ensure that debts submitted are genuine, enforceable, and not subject to undisclosed disputes or set-offs;
  • Pay Credvanta’s commission and fees in accordance with the Terms & Conditions.

6. Fees & Payment

Fees and commission are payable in accordance with Credvanta’s Terms & Conditions (clause 3). Commission becomes due upon recovery of any funds from the Debtor. Credvanta invoices are payable within 7 calendar days of the invoice date unless otherwise agreed in writing.

7. Data Protection

Each party agrees to comply with its obligations under applicable data protection law. Credvanta will process Debtor personal data as a data processor on behalf of the Client. The Client acts as the data controller in respect of Debtor personal data and warrants that it has a lawful basis for sharing such data with Credvanta.

8. Liability

Credvanta’s liability under this Agreement is limited as set out in the Terms & Conditions (clause 15). Credvanta shall not be liable for any indirect or consequential loss. The Client indemnifies Credvanta as set out in clause 14 of the Terms & Conditions.

9. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement on written notice. Termination does not affect any commission or fees properly due at the time of termination. Post-termination commission obligations apply as set out in the Terms & Conditions (clause 18).

10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Scotland. Any dispute shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Scottish courts.

Credvanta Recovery Group Limited (Company No. 17251971) undertakes commercial business-to-business (B2B) debt recovery activity only. Credvanta does not undertake regulated consumer debt collection activity and is not authorised or regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA).
