Our Services

Four ways we help investors access the North East property market — each deal researched, verified and ready to act on.

Buy-to-Let
Service 01

Buy-to-Let Sourcing

We source single-let properties across the North East that deliver reliable monthly rental income. Every deal is researched for rental demand, yield potential and local market conditions before it reaches you.

Whether you're a first-time landlord or adding to an existing portfolio, we identify properties that perform — and handle the groundwork so you can make decisions with confidence.

  • Rental yield analysis on every deal
  • Tenant demand research by postcode
  • Full due diligence support provided
  • Suitable for cash buyers and mortgage purchasers

HMO Sourcing
Service 02

HMO Sourcing

HMOs (Houses in Multiple Occupation) offer some of the strongest cash flows available in residential property. We identify suitable properties, assess licensing requirements and present opportunities that stack up financially.

Our HMO sourcing covers everything from article 4 area checks to room-by-room income projections — giving you the clarity to move quickly on the right deals.

  • Licensing compliance check included
  • Room-by-room yield calculation
  • Refurbishment cost estimates provided
  • Suitable for experienced and first-time HMO investors

Below Market Value
Service 03

Below Market Value Deals

Buying below market value means you start with equity in your pocket. We work with motivated sellers and distressed assets to bring you properties priced under their true market value.

Every BMV deal we source is independently verified — so you know the discount is real and the numbers hold up under scrutiny. These opportunities move fast; register your interest to be first in line.

  • Instant equity on purchase
  • Ideal for refinancing and BRRR strategies
  • Verified valuations provided
  • Limited availability — first come, first served

Development Opportunities
Service 04

Development Opportunities

From small refurbishment projects to larger development sites, we identify properties with genuine uplift potential. Perfect for investors looking to manufacture value rather than just buy and hold.

We assess planning potential, provide comparable sales data and — where appropriate — consider joint venture structures. North East focus with national connections.

  • Planning potential assessed upfront
  • Comparable sales data provided
  • JV opportunities considered
  • North East focus with national connections
