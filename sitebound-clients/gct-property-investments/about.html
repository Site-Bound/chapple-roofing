Who We Are

Built on Local Knowledge, Driven by Results

GCT Property Investments Ltd is a North East-based property sourcing company built on one simple principle: finding the right deal for the right investor. We work with buyers across the UK and overseas to identify high-quality investment opportunities in one of the UK's most affordable and high-yielding property markets.

Whether you're a seasoned portfolio landlord or making your first investment, we take the time to understand your goals and source deals that genuinely work for you. No generic listings. No wasted viewings. Just targeted, researched opportunities delivered directly to you.

We specialise in buy-to-let, HMO, below market value and development opportunities — covering Newcastle, Sunderland, Gateshead, Middlesbrough and the wider North East region. Our connections with local agents, solicitors and mortgage brokers mean we can support you from initial enquiry all the way through to completion.
