What We Do

Three pillars. One goal.
A better Wakefield.

Every penny of the BID levy is reinvested directly into the city centre — improving the environment, driving footfall, and protecting your business.

Place

The Physical City

Street Rangers deep-cleaning pavements, Wakefield in Bloom floral displays, and ongoing environmental improvements that make the city centre a place people want to be.

Promotion

Getting the Word Out

Festivals, events, and the Ey Up Wakefield consumer marketing campaign — bringing visitors in and putting Wakefield businesses in front of the audiences that matter.

Provision

Support for Members

Free meeting rooms, 200+ live training courses through our city-centre learning hub, and direct business support — the tools your team needs, at no extra cost.

Key Initiatives

Making a difference,
every single day.

From dawn cleaning crews to late-night radio networks, Wakefield BID operates across the full life of the city centre.

Business Crime Reduction Partnership

Safer Wakefield

90+ city-centre businesses connected through Hytera digital radios, a real-time intelligence app, and 24/7 support — working alongside West Yorkshire Police and Wakefield Council to keep crime at its lowest level in four years.

Daily Operations

Street Rangers

Our dedicated team is on the ground every morning — deep-cleaning pavements, removing graffiti, clearing spillages and maintaining the environment that our businesses depend on.

Consumer Marketing

Ey Up Wakefield

Our citywide consumer brand — promoting member businesses through campaigns, social media, and a unified identity that gives Wakefield's independent economy a voice.

City Environment

Wakefield in Bloom

Seasonal planters and floral installations positioned throughout the city centre — small details that make a measurable difference to how visitors experience Wakefield.

Member Benefit · Free

City Centre Learning Hub

Over 200 free, live training courses available exclusively to BID levy payers. Customer service, conflict resolution, digital skills, first aid — everything your team needs to grow.
